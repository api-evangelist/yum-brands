# Yum Brands

Yum! Brands is one of the world's largest restaurant companies, with global brands including KFC, Pizza Hut, Taco Bell, and The Habit Burger Grill. The company operates over 63,000 restaurants in 155 countries and territories, with approximately 90% franchise-operated.

## Byte by Yum! Technology Platform

Yum! Brands launched **Byte by Yum!** in 2025 — a proprietary AI-driven SaaS platform powering digital ordering, point-of-sale, kitchen operations, menu management, inventory management, and labor management across all four brands. As of 2026, the platform processes over 300 million digital transactions annually in the US and has deployed to 25,000+ restaurants globally.

## APIs

### Byte by Yum! Platform

The Byte by Yum! platform powers digital experiences across all Yum! Brands restaurants. While no public REST API is available, the platform includes:

- Digital ordering and mobile apps
- Point-of-sale systems
- Kitchen operations and display systems
- Menu management
- Inventory and labor management
- Customer loyalty programs
